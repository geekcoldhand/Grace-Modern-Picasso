About 👩🏽‍🎓

Kiana 'Grace' Harris. is an emerging talented neo-expressionisum artist. She has been working with hues and pigments for decades producing artwork with intuitive flair! Educated in the field of psychology at the University of Tennessee, Grace expanded her mind to express the revelation of mental chaos. Applying her knowledge mind she can produce wildly detailed and colorful highly skilled artworks that leave its viewers floored. Experience, reflect, and enjoy the modern Picasso.

Connections 🌐

Her lifesize works, featured in open galleries and fashion shows, have helped create feelings of chaotic control where she loves to meet and greet other fellow artists and art appreciative(s). Currently, she is working out of her studio in Roswell, Georgia, where she spends most of her time crafting her detailed extensions of neo-expressionism.
