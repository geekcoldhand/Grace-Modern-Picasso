Neo-Now

What is Neo-Now?

Neo-now - describes the current cultural state of the neo-expressionist art movement. In the late 70s, the art movement gained immense popularity by names like Basquiat and Julian Schnabel. But what is neo-expressionism- you might ask? It can be characterized by intense subjectivity as if you entered another world. In the world, an intellectualized application of contrast, texture, and large-scale narratives draws the viewer. The ambiguous subjects of the art alienate feelings of comfort or familiarity with an underlined invisible weirdness. This exchange leaves feelings of interpretation open to exploration!
